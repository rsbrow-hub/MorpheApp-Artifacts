## 📲 How to Install

1. **Download MicroG** (if not rooted) from the [official source](https://github.com/MorpheApp/MicroG-RE/releases).
2. **Get the latest APK** from our [Releases page](https://github.com/rsbrow-hub/MorpheApp-Artifacts/releases).
3. Enable **"Install from unknown sources"** in your device settings.
4. Install the APK, sign in with your Google account via MicroG, and you’re all set.

> ⚠️ Google Play Protect may show a warning – click **"Install anyway"**. The APKs are safe but not signed by the original developer.
