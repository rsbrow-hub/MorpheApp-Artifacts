## 📲 How to Install (Complete Guide)

### ✅ Pre‑requisites
- Android 7.0 or newer
- Enable **Install from unknown sources** in Settings → Security (or Settings → Apps → Special access)

### 📌 Step‑by‑step
1. **Install MicroG** (skip if your device is rooted)
   - Download MicroG APK from the link above.
   - Install it and grant any requested permissions.

2. **Download MorpheApp APK**
   - Go to the [Releases page](https://github.com/rsbrow-hub/MorpheApp-Artifacts/releases/latest) and get the latest YouTube or Music APK.

3. **Install the APK**
   - Open the downloaded file and tap **Install**.
   - If Google Play Protect shows a warning, click **Install anyway** – the APK is safe but not signed by Google.

4. **Sign In**
   - Open MorpheApp and sign in with your Google account via MicroG.
   - You’re now ad‑free and ready to go.

After installing, our settings file will unlock everything automatically. Here’s exactly where to apply it inside the app:

1. **Download the settings file** from the button above.
2. Open **MorpheApp** (YouTube).

5. Inside Miscellaneous, scroll down again and tap **Import/Export**.
